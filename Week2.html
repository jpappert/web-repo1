Left Right Tim

Premiere Comedy Improv

Left Right Tim is a premiere improv team on campus, award winning and has been around 14 years!.
We have shows every Friday in Hale 270, and are hoping that we have a good and productive preformance year!.
We have a variety of shows, ranging from Improv Structures to more wild shows like musicals and a halloween show!
Left Right Tim provides friendships, community, and an outlet for art.

When joining an improv team on campus, you open yourself up to new and exciting opportunities.
At Left Right Tim, we cultivate an inclusive and heartfelt community. And is a great way to grow and learn!

Left Right Tim is a wonderful way to involve yourself in the community. We have auditions September 14/15!
